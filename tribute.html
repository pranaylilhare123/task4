Tribute to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

The People's President and Visionary Scientist

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, known as the "Missile Man of India," was a brilliant scientist and a beloved President. His contributions to science and his vision for India's future continue to inspire millions around the world.

Notable Achievements

  • Played a key role in India's Pokhran-II nuclear tests
  • Authored several influential books, including "Wings of Fire" and "Ignited Minds"
  • Served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007
  • Honored with the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award
